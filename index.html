The Band


Hot Mass are an alternative punk band from South Wales, UK With members originally carving their way in the music scene as young rockers from previous UK bands Hailing from Swansea, UK,

  • Rhys Jenkins Guitar / Vocals
  • Chris O'Reilly Bass / Vocals
  • Scott Neill Guitar
  • Duncan Hamill Drums

The Sound


Hot Mass play crushing riffs like they arrived into the world with electric guitars in their hands.
With the current state of the world, what better time to listen to punk.
Their eclectic taste in music has all been thrown into the melting pot to create some melodic, grunge fuelled, indie popping, jaw dropping, punk bangers awash with defiant upbeat lyricism and more guitar battles than youâ€™d find on a Thin Lizzy record.

The Future


In the immediate future, Hot Mass intend to release singles and music videos to promote the upcoming album.
Ahead of the release, the music/band will be promoted through a creative social media PR and marketing campaign, organising new designs for merchandise, and finally, by engaging with established as well as new booking agents to secure touring opportunities.
